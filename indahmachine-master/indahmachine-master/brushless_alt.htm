PRODUCT FAMILIES
 MEDIUM FREQ BRUSHLESS SYNCH ALTERNATOR  

Main character and performance:

1.   the wave form of output voltage is stable, from no-load to full-load, the total harmonic capacity of phase voltage wave form is not more than 3%, each harmonic capacity is not more than 2%, deviation is nor more than 4%

2.    Voltage modulation is not more than 1%;

3.    At 15% asymmetrical load, the unbalance of 3-phase voltage is not more than 2%; At 25% asymmetrical load, the unbalance of 3-phase voltage is not more than 5%;

4.   At the asymmetrical load phase shift on null point of the 3-phase voltage wave form shall be within 120 +/- 2 degrees;

5.   The stable voltage regulating ratio is nor more than +/- 1%;

6.   The transient voltage regulating ratio at revulsion rated power load is not more than +/- 15% or +/- 20%, the stable time is not more than 0.5s;
